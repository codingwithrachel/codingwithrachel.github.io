Hi, I'm Rachel.

If you have somehow stumbled into this place, welcome!

Currently, I'm a developer working in the arts out of New York, New York.

Before I came to join the tech scene in the Big Apple in the Spring of 2015, I was in Seoul, Korea working in education. Before that, I was completing my undergrad studies in International Studies at Duke University in Durham, NC.

New York has been very good to me. I've met amazing people at local meetups, conferences, study groups, and work. What got me into the tech industry and have continued to fascinate me, are the open sharing of code and ideas (you will want to share your hard work with me?!) and the creative problem-solving nature of software development.

These days, when I am not using AngularJS at work, I am learning HTML5 Canvas games, BackboneJS, ReactJS, and d3.js.

As with coding, my love goes out to snowboarding, playing keyboard, watching basketball & soccer, puppies, dark chocolate, and coffee (yes, that's a coffee bean up there in the tab in case you were wondering!).
